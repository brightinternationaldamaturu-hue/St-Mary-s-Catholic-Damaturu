About St. Mary Catholic Church
St. Mary Catholic Church, Damaturu Parish, is a vibrant community of faith dedicated to worship, service, and spiritual growth. Founded on the teachings of Jesus Christ and guided by the traditions of the Catholic Church, the parish serves as a place of prayer, unity, and hope for all who seek God.

As a parish entrusted to the care of the Blessed Virgin Mary, we strive to imitate her humility, obedience, and unwavering faith. Through the celebration of the Holy Mass, the Sacraments, and various pastoral activities, we nurture the spiritual lives of our members and strengthen our bond as one family in Christ.

Our church is not only a place of worship but also a center for charity, evangelization, and community development. We welcome everyone—young and old, visitors and parishioners alike—to join us in praising God and growing together in love and faith.
